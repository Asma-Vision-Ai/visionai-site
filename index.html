Questions? We've Got Answers

How long does implementation take?
Typical onboarding is 1–3 weeks depending on site complexity; pilot deployments can start within 48 hours.
Do we need to replace existing cameras?
No. Vision AI works with most IP cameras and common analog setups via edge adapters.
How is data stored and secured?
Metadata is stored encrypted in the cloud; raw video can be configured to remain on-premise or removed after anonymization.
Can it integrate with our current systems?
Yes. Our APIs, webhooks, and SIEM connectors allow integration with most incident and operations platforms.
What kind of support do you provide?
24/7 support for all plans, dedicated onboarding for enterprise customers.
Is there a minimum contract period?
We offer flexible monthly plans and annual agreements for volume-based discounts.
How accurate is the AI detection?
Accuracy varies by use case; our models provide confidence values and can be tuned per camera. We continuously validate with client data.
What happens if internet connection drops?
Edge devices buffer events locally and sync once connectivity returns; critical local alerts can still be triggered.
